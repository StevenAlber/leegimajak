Why this nameMiks see nimi

Leegi is the flameMajak is the lighthouse

Leegi on leekMajak on majakas

I chose this name because it says exactly what I mean. The flame is the part of you that is still alive — still warm, still real — even when everything around you has gone cold or loud. The lighthouse is what happens when that flame becomes steady enough to be seen.

I am not here to teach you how to shine. I believe you already know. You may have just forgotten — under the weight of doing, performing, and being what everyone else needs you to be.

Ma valisin selle nime, sest see ütleb täpselt seda, mida mõtlen. Leek on see osa sinust, mis on ikka elus — ikka soe, ikka päris — isegi siis, kui kõik sinu ümber on külmaks või valjuks muutunud. Majakas on see, mis juhtub, kui see leek muutub piisavalt kindlaks, et teda nähakse.

Ma ei ole siin selleks, et sulle õpetada, kuidas paista. Ma usun, et sa juba tead. Sa oled lihtsalt unustanud — tegemiste, etendamise ja kõigi teiste vajaduste raskuse all.

I

Beauty that nourishes

Not curated perfection. Real beauty — the kind you find in morning light, in food made with hands, in a dress that makes you feel like yourself again.

II

Truth without harshness

Honesty that doesn't break you down. Reflection that opens doors instead of pointing at wounds. Seeing yourself clearly and liking what you see.

III

Aliveness without performance

Not the kind you post about. The kind you feel when you stand in the sea, cook something that smells right, or sit in silence and actually want to be there.

I

Ilu, mis toidab

Mitte kureeritud täiuslikkus. Päris ilu — selline, mida leiad hommikuses valguses, kätega tehtud toidus, kleidis, mis paneb sind taas iseendana tundma.

II

Tõde ilma karmuseta

Ausus, mis ei lõhu sind. Peegeldus, mis avab uksi, mitte ei osuta haavadele. Enda selgelt nägemine ja meeldimine sellele, mida näed.

III

Elavus ilma etenduseta

Mitte selline, millest postitad. Selline, mida tunned, kui seisad meres, teed midagi, mis lõhnab õigesti, või istud vaikuses ja tahad tegelikult seal olla.

Not advice. Not a programA place that feels like coming home to yourself

Mitte nõuandeid. Mitte programmiKoht, mis tundub nagu enese juurde tagasi tulek

I share what I see, taste, feel, and live through — honestly, without filters, without performance. Food that makes me grateful. Places that make me breathe deeper. Thoughts that arrive in the morning and won't leave me alone. Moments with my children that stop time.

This is not content. This is a life — shared with the kind of attention that turns ordinary moments into something you can feel in your chest.

Ma jagan seda, mida näen, maitsun, tunnen ja läbi elan — ausalt, ilma filtriteta, ilma etenduseta. Toitu, mis paneb mind tänulikuks. Kohti, mis panevad mind sügavamalt hingama. Mõtteid, mis saabuvad hommikul ega jäta mind rahule. Hetki lastega, mis peatavad aja.

See ei ole sisu. See on elu — jagatud sellise tähelepanuga, mis muudab tavalised hetked millekski, mida sa tunned rindkeres.

"I spent years dimming myself to fit rooms that were never built for me. This place is the room I finally built for myself — and for every woman who is tired of shrinking."
"Ma veetsin aastaid ennast tuhmimaks tehes, et mahtuda ruumidesse, mis ei olnud kunagi minu jaoks ehitatud. See koht on ruum, mille ma lõpuks endale ehitasin — ja igale naisele, kes on kahanemisest väsinud."
