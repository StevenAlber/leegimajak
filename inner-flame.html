What this space holds

Mida see ruum hoiab

This is not a course. There is nothing to complete, no grade to earn, no transformation to perform. The Inner Flame Circle is simply a quieter, warmer, more intimate space — where the letters go deeper, the reflections become more personal, and the pace belongs entirely to you.

See ei ole kursus. Siin pole midagi lõpetada, hinnet saada ega muutumist etendada. Sisemise leegi ring on lihtsalt vaiksem, soojem, intiimsem ruum — kus kirjad lähevad sügavamaks, mõtisklused muutuvad isiklikumaks ja tempo kuulub ainult sulle.

You will find here what the open Lighthouse Letter begins — but continued with more honesty, more stillness, and more room to breathe.

Siin leiad selle, mida avatud Majaka kiri alustab — aga jätkatud suurema aususe, vaikuse ja hingamisruumiga.

What arrives each month

Mis saabub iga kuu

  • Two deeper letters — longer, more intimate reflections on inner life, seasons, and the quiet courage of being alive
  • Kaks sügavamat kirja — pikemad, intiimsemad mõtisklused sisemisest elust, hooaegadest ja vaiksest julgusest elada
  • Seasonal ritual guide — a gentle practice for each solstice and equinox, grounded in nature and presence
  • Hooajaline rituaalijuhis — õrn praktika igaks pööripäevaks, juurdunud looduses ja kohalolus
  • A private space — access to a quiet community of women who share this warmth, without noise or performance
  • Privaatne ruum — ligipääs vaiksele naiste kogukonnale, kes jagavad seda soojust ilma müra ja etendamiseta
  • Early access — everything new from Leegi Majak reaches you first
  • Varajane ligipääs — kõik uus Leegi Majakist jõuab sinuni esimesena

Who this is for

Kellele see on

This is for the woman who already knows she doesn't need fixing. She knows her light hasn't gone out — it may have dimmed, but it's still there. She doesn't need another program. She needs a place that feels like coming home to herself.

See on naisele, kes juba teab, et ta ei vaja parandamist. Ta teab, et tema valgus pole kustunud — see võib olla tuhmunud, aga on ikka alles. Ta ei vaja järjekordset programmi. Ta vajab kohta, mis tundub nagu kojutulek iseenda juurde.

If the Lighthouse Letter made you feel something — a warmth, a recognition, a quiet "yes" — then this circle is where that feeling has room to grow.

Kui Majaka kiri pani sind midagi tundma — soojust, äratundmist, vaikset "jah" — siis see ring on koht, kus sellel tundel on ruumi kasvada.
